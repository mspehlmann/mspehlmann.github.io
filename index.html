Discovery Optimization

Launch 2024

Experts in efficient first pass geo recon.

Discoveries are at all time lows. Relatedly exploration is highly focused on near mine targets with little interests in higher risk first pass geo programs. MundoX beleives that high risk first pass programs are the only way to make substantial new discoveries. There is currently little interest in this type of exploration. The current green field model is an established miner will contract out the work to a third party contractor whose only asset is the independent contractors they higher. This approach requires substantial, costly oversight from the miner. MundoX believes that the a systematic, lighwieght, self sustaining approach will yeild greater returns than hiring a field crew at premium and deploying the largely untrained crews to an area with notebook, maps, and compass.

MundoX, the higher grade option in exploration

MundoX is currently positioning itself to deliver on budget, high performance first pass geological exploration. Our combination of field ops expertise and proprietary data collection technology will produce a deliverable that will plug into a miner's existing database. All field crews have extensive experience working throughout the most inhospitable terrain in Alaska. We operate light and efficient forgoing the need for expensive camp facilities allowing for greater exploration coverage over a season.
