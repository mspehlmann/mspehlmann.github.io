Spatial Simulation

This is an adaption of a conditional spatial simulation that I originally wrote in Python and adpated somewhat faithfully to JavaScript. The simualtion uses a Guassian process convolution to simulate a categorical spatial process. This is a component of a larger project that studies the capabilites of process convolutions to estimate a continuous response conditioned on a categorical process. I am particularity interested in applying this methodology to scenarios that break the stationarity assumption in Kriging. Right now this work shows promise in allowing faster model fitting in larger spatial domains without the need for careful sub domaining to meet the staionarity assumption. Please reach out to me if you are interested in this work. The current repository is located here Conditioned Simulation
